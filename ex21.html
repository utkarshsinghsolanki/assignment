Banasthali Vidyapith, established in 1935, is the world's largest residential university for women, doing pioneering work in its field for more than eight decades. The 'Banasthali story' has no parallel across the globe. It originated in only of its kind situation when a father lost his promising daughter before its hour and decided to train other girls the same way. Know More >
